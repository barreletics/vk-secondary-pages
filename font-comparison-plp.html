Locked rules

What does not change across variants

  • Pricing: DM Sans, weight 500, var(--near-black) — never Playfair on numbers.
  • Brand line under product: 12px mid grey — same across PLP columns.
  • Badges: same uppercase / tracking pattern as the category PLP grid.

What you are choosing

Product name line only

  • A — Grid default: highest scan speed; neutral commerce tone.
  • B — Featured row: matches homepage / editorial product cards; more “boutique” in longer titles.
  • C — Display compact: Playfair one step smaller for 4-up grids without switching to full sans.
