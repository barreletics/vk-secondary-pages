Frequently Asked Questions

VK Credit Card FAQ

Can I use a Synchrony card from another store at Vintage King?
No. The only Synchrony Bank issued credit card you can use to finance a Vintage King purchase is the Vintage King credit card. Cards issued by Synchrony for other retailers cannot be used at Vintage King.
Does promotional financing apply to used and vintage gear?
Promotional financing (6–48 month offers) applies to new items from select brands only. Used, vintage, B-stock, and Demo Deal items are excluded from promotional financing but can be purchased with the card at the standard APR.
Will checking if I prequalify affect my credit score?
No. Pre-qualification uses a soft inquiry that does not impact your credit score. A hard inquiry only occurs if you proceed with a full application after pre-qualifying.
How do I manage my existing VK Credit Card account?
Sign in at mysynchrony.com to view your balance, make payments, check promotional financing details, and manage your account. You can also contact Synchrony customer service directly for account questions.
Can I combine financing with other promotions?
Promotional financing cannot be combined with other discounts or promotions. The qualifying purchase amount must be on one receipt. Contact an Audio Consultant at 888.653.1184 for details on current offers.
