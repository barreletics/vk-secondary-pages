Overview

Make Professional Recordings at Home

Whether you're building out your first bedroom studio or constructing a professionally-designed control room in your basement, it's possible to make high-quality recordings, mixes and masters from the comfort of your own home. The key is choosing the right gear for your budget, your room, and your workflow.

Vintage King has helped thousands of engineers and musicians build project studios that punch well above their weight. From a simple interface-and-mic setup to a fully outfitted mixing room with outboard processing, we can guide you through every stage of the process.
