Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Live Sound

Everything you need to know about working with Vintage King on your live sound project.

What types of venues does Vintage King support for live sound installations?
We support a wide range of venues including houses of worship, concert halls, clubs, fitness studios, corporate event spaces, and portable PA setups for touring acts. Our team designs custom solutions tailored to the acoustic characteristics and audience capacity of each space.
Do you handle the full installation or just equipment sales?
We offer end-to-end service: from initial acoustic assessment and system design through equipment procurement, installation, calibration, and staff training. We can also supply equipment only if you have your own installation team or integrator.
Can Vintage King help with video and streaming integration?
Yes. Many of our live sound projects include video recording, live streaming, and projection components. We design integrated AV systems that cover audio, multi-camera video capture, live streaming to platforms like YouTube and Facebook, and in-venue projection and display systems.
What brands does Vintage King carry for live sound?
We carry all major professional live sound brands including Yamaha, QSC, Shure, Sennheiser, Pioneer DJ, Allen and Heath, JBL, Electro-Voice, and many more. Our consultants help match the right equipment to your venue requirements and budget.
