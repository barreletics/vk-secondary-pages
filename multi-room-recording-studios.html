FAQ

Common questions.

If you do not see your question here, request a discovery call above.

How long does a multi-room studio build take?
Most multi-room builds run 6–14 months from spec to first session. Acoustic shell construction sets the long pole; gear specification, pre-racking, and signal-flow design run in parallel so the room is ready to operate the day construction completes.
Do you handle acoustics or just gear?
Both. We design with vetted acoustician partners and then specify monitoring, treatment, signal flow, and gear so the room and the gear match. Acoustician coordination is included in the design phase.
Can you work with our existing architect or GC?
Yes. We routinely integrate with outside architects and general contractors. We deliver gear lists, rack drawings, signal-flow documentation, and conduit specs your team can build to.
What does the budget typically cover?
Gear, integration, install labor, calibration, and post-launch support. Construction, acoustic shell, and HVAC are typically separate line items handled by the GC and acoustician.
Do you offer financing for the build?
Yes. VK Credit Card financing and Section 179 tax deductions are both options for qualifying purchases. A consultant will walk you through the right structure during discovery.
What support do you offer after install?
Every spec'd component carries the VK Warranty. The Tech Shop is on call for service, and your account team stays in place for ongoing gear additions and upgrades.
