Home Microphones Neumann U 87 Ai

The Neumann U 87 Ai is the industry standard large-diaphragm condenser microphone. For over 50 years, it has been the first choice of recording engineers worldwide for vocals, acoustic instruments, and broadcast applications.

Featuring three directional characteristics — omnidirectional, cardioid, and figure-8 — and switchable 10 dB pad and high-pass filter, the U 87 Ai delivers the clarity and presence that have made it the most recorded microphone in history.
