Category page family — pick by intent.
Four numbered variants. Same category, different hero. Pick by goal: conversion, curation, brand spotlight, or simple authority.

4 Dark strip — authority, no photo Dark band, no image Dark Category authority without a feature item. Cleanest dark option. Headline + CTA only. View →
Rule — when to go dark on a category page

Default = light. Use dark only for: (a) commerce category pages where the brand needs authority, (b) co-op / partnered campaign weeks, or (c) campaign drops where a single hero photo carries the page. Never use dark on About, Careers, Help, FAQ, or any service / storytelling page.
